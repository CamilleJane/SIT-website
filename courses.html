Our Courses

Courses or program offered by School of Information and Technology

BS Information Technology

Bachelor of Science and Technology focuses on the study of both hardware and software technologies involving planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing and administrating, and maintaining information technology infrastructure that provide computing solutions to address the need of an organization.

BS Computer Science

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science provides the study of computing concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations, and new developments in computing. The program prepares student to design and create algorithmically complex software and develop new and effective algorithms for solving computer problems.

BS Information System

Bachelor of Science in Information System provides student withi foundation knowledge on organizations and Information and Communication Technology (ICT). It allows student to understand how ICT can enable organizations to improve performance and achieve competitive advantage. It trains student to develop strong analytical, technical, and leadership skills to thrive on global environment as future information system professionals
